Biology - Career & Opportunities

About Biology

Biology is the study of living organisms, including their structure, function, growth, evolution, and interactions with their environment.

Streams in Biology

Biology is divided into various streams, including:

  • Botany (Study of Plants)
  • Zoology (Study of Animals)
  • Microbiology
  • Genetics
  • Biotechnology
  • Ecology

Career Opportunities in Biology

Biology offers a wide range of career options in research, healthcare, and environmental sciences:

  • Biomedical Scientist
  • Geneticist
  • Ecologist
  • Pharmacologist
  • Biotechnologist
  • Wildlife Biologist

High-Paying Jobs in Biology

Some of the best-paying jobs for biology graduates include:

  • Medical Scientist
  • Pharmaceutical Researcher
  • Genetic Engineer
  • Biotechnologist
  • Neuroscientist
