About Our School

Himalaya Higher Secondary School, located in the village of Neori, is committed to delivering high-quality education to students of all backgrounds. With a focus on academic excellence and holistic development, we offer state-of-the-art classrooms, well-equipped laboratories. Our experienced faculty ensures that students receive the best education while also encouraging them to engage in extracurricular activities.

We believe in nurturing creativity, leadership, and discipline among our students. Our school provides various academic programs, including Science, Commerce, and Biology, ensuring a comprehensive learning experience. At Himalaya Higher Secondary School, we prepare students for a successful future by fostering a strong educational foundation combined with values and ethics.
