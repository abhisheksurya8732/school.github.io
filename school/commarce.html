Commerce Stream

Commerce is one of the most popular streams among students after 10th grade. It deals with trade, business, accounting, and financial studies. It is an ideal choice for students who have an interest in numbers, business strategies, and market dynamics.

Subjects in Commerce Stream

  • Accountancy
  • Business Studies
  • Economics
  • Mathematics / Informatics Practices
  • English
  • Entrepreneurship (Optional)

Career Options After Commerce

Commerce stream opens doors to various career opportunities in different sectors such as finance, business, banking, and government services. Below are some of the top career options:

  • Chartered Accountant (CA)
  • Company Secretary (CS)
  • Banking & Finance
  • Investment Banking
  • Business Management (MBA)
  • Digital Marketing
  • Stock Market Analyst
  • Economist

Job Opportunities in Commerce

  • Accountant
  • Financial Analyst
  • Tax Consultant
  • HR Manager
  • Marketing Manager
  • Auditor
  • Government Jobs (UPSC, SSC, Banking)
